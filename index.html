    We are UAB SciTech Honors.

    Our mission is “To prepare scientific leaders of the next generation by engaging students in interdisciplinary classroom, leadership, research, and innovation experiences which culminate in the dissemination of new scientific knowledge.” But what does that mean?

    Critical Thinking and Leadership

    Every SciTech Honors class encourages our students to think as leaders in scientific thought. We develop next generation scientists and innovators who think critically and become leaders to help improve the greater community.

    Leading Faculty and Educators

    Our students learn from some of the best faculty at UAB and across the world. Students have access to some of the leading minds in scientific thought and research.

    Research and Innovation

    SciTech students have unparalleled access to research opportunities at UAB. On average, SciTech students start research earlier than other students. Many of our students have been first-authored, published multiple research papers, and have presented at international scientific conferences.

    Check out some of our Opportunities

    Research, Publications, and Presentations

    Our students get early experience in the world of scientific research, which ends up giving them a competitive advantage in the workplace, graduate, or professional school.

    Leadership

    We teach our students to think critically and embrace their role as leaders in the classroom, campus, and greater scientific community. We provide the tools to allow our students to do so.

    Unique SciTech Honors Courses

    Only SciTech students have access to unique courses. Create a new campus organization in Primetime Leadership, or develop a new piece of biotechnology in Engineering Research Methods.

    Access to Faculty

    Not only will you be taught by some of UAB's best professors, but your role as a SciTech student gives you a competitive access to laboratory PIs and other scientific opportunities on campus.

    Early Course Registration

    As an honors student, you will recieve early registration for courses. This allows you to get the classes you want, with the professors you want, with the time schedule you want.

    A Supportive Community

    As the smallest and most tight-knight honors program on campus, students are thrust into a community that is not only supportive, but fun: from our first-year retreat to our annual holiday shindig.

    Frequently Asked Questions


    What type of student is the SciTech Honors Program looking for?

    A student who is curious, resourceful, ready to be an innovator, ready to explore new ways of thinking, interested in research, and helpful to his/her peers.

    What are the course requirements for STH and the Honors College?

    Please refer to this page here.

    What is research like and why is it so important?

    Scientific research generates new knowledge. It's the foundation for advances in medicine and in the technology that supports human existence and human progress.

    What type of time commitment is STH?

    STH classes take time and dedication as will any other class. Many students become strongly committed to their research and choose to devote significant time to this.
