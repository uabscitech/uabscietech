Our Signature Honors Courses

Classes with Meaning.

As a SciTech student, you'll have access to exclusive and specialized classes not available to other students. For example, you can create a new campus organization in Primetime Leadership, or develop a new piece of biotechnology in Engineering Research Methods. Whatever your passion is, you can find a class in the SciTech curricula that fits your needs.

Purpose-driven courses

We understand that a lot of our students join the SciTech program to learn the skills they need to become effective STEM experts Therefore, our courses are catered to fit their goals: for example, the SciTech English 202 class teaches students about effective communication in research papers and public-facing science documents.

Discussion-based learning

SciTech is one of the most competitive programs on campus, making us the smallest honors college subdivision. This allows our students to participate in Discussion-based seminar classes, which fosters critical thinking and learning from their peers.

Real-world applications

The end goal of every SciTech class is to prepare our students to become effective thinkers in the sciences. Whether you are going into medicine, engineering, computer science, or just about any STEM-related field, the SciTech curricula will prepare you to enter your respective field.
