Our Close-Knit Community

Your Classmates. Your Best Friends.

The Science and Technology Honors Program prides itself on being one of the only honors programs on campus that truly considers themself as a community instead of a "program". Unlike other honors programs, the people around you aren't just your "classmates" - they are some of the closest friends you will make at UAB.

A Close Knit Community

As one of the most competitive honors programs on campus, we are also the smallest. This allows you to truly meet every single person in the SciTech Honors Program, and meet the other classes that came before you. Your first SciTech seminar will be with everyone in your class! As a result, we are a very close-knit community.

Fun Events and Programs

All incoming SciTech freshmen get to participate in the overnight SciTech retreat at Camp McDowell, full of fun and games. Additionally, we host a holiday shindig every year, where the entire SciTech community gets together for laughter and fun.

The Peer Mentoring System

Every SciTech freshman gets to choose a peer mentoring "family" at the beginning of the year. You peer mentors are older SciTech students who will help you through any situation, or your family will get together to do something fun. They become some of your best friends through your years at UAB!
