Teaching our Students to be Leaders and Crtical Thinkers

Shaping the Leaders of Tomorrow

We recognize that in today's world, it's not just enough to be a "scientist", and "engineer", a "physician". Rather, it's important to become a leader in your field. That's why at SciTech, we shape our students into the leaders and critical thinkers of tomorrow.

Leadership

We teach our students to become servant leaders from day one. We teach and encourage leadership in the classroom, on campus, and in the greater community. A large part of SciTech focuses on leadership development in addition to science and research.

Critical Thinking

Much of science in the real world is more about application. Memorizing a textbook in the classroom isn't going to help in the operation room, in the laboratory, or in the business meeting. Therefore, we don't ask our students to memorize - rather, we teach them how to think for themselves.

Professional Development

In addition to becoming educated thinkers and leaders, we also recognize the importance of professional development. Much of the introductor SciTech Honors Seminars have a large emphasis on becoming professionals in an academic and business setting.
